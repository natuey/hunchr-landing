What is Hunchr?

Hunchr is an innovative educational platform in trading index futures, cryptocurrencies and forex.

At Hunchr, our interactive games simulate the real trading market. Practice how to trade futures, cryptos and forex under real market conditions. The educational game let you buy those instruments in a risk-free way as well as teach you how to manage your money.

We also developed several educational courses designed to teach the basics of how to trade futures, cryptos and forex. You will gain on how to build and implement trading techniques to ensure your success in real life investment.
